Published on September 10th, 2025 by Olamide Akinleye

This article investigated the possible effects of curcumin on the cardiovascular functions

Key Concepts:

The effects of curcumin were examined in heart rate, systolic and diastolic blood pressures

Conclusions:

Curcumin may have the potential antioxidant functions which generally improves the cardiovascular functions
